what is this?

bunnyclub is a cs:go server, it's core difference from others being it's more movement-based community!

the reason bunico created bunnyclub is due to the lack of just a GOOD 5v5 cs:go server. the only ones that are out there are dead, toxic, hvh, or multiple of those. but look no further! this is the last cs:go server you'll need to play on.

the server's content is primarily community-driven! we will always listen to your feedback in the discord server! but, if you don't want to join it, that's okay. just contact flynn!

how does this still work?

when you click on counter-strike 2's properties, and go into betas, you'll find a beta titled csgo_legacy. launch this, and you can still play cs:go offline! expectedly, official servers are not supported anymore, so that's why this exists! some features may not work on launch, like your inventory.

if you wish to access your inventory in game, among other things, open your cs2's local files, go to csgo\steam.inf, open with notepad and edit the "ClientVersion" and "ServerVersion" to 2000258, after that, your inventory and community browser (which has nearly zero cs:go servers on it) will work ingame! obviously, any items made after cs2's release will not show.

how do i join the server?

when you get whitelisted, you'll be able to see the ip. just copy the text from the discord or steam group, and put it in your console! (it won't let you join if you're not whitelisted, so i wouldn't reccomend trying.)

if you want to go the extra mile, something like bind ] "connect (server)" would be a lot faster, and you won't have to tab out.

FAQ

is the server always on?

unfortunately, no. as of writing this, you can only ask an admin to start the server for you. we are working on a solution.

if you have any idea on how to fix this, we'd love your help! our server is focused on being community-driven, so support is appreciated, but not necessary.

why am i getting stuck on downloading a map?

for some unknown reason, csgo_legacy servers do not permit downloading of maps while in the server. to avoid getting stuck like this, download this .zip and move it's contents to ...\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\csgo\maps.
